(b) Pelo teste da razão:

$\displaystyle \left|\frac{a_{n 1}}{a_{n}}\right|=\left| \frac{\frac{\left(n 1\right)^{2}}{2^{n 1}}}{\frac{n^{2}}{2^{n}}}\right|=\frac{(n 1)^2}{2^n\cdot2}\cdot\frac{2^n}{n^2}=\frac{(n 1)^2}{2n^2}=\frac{n^2 2n 1}{2n^2}$

Daí,

$\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n^2 2n 1}{2n^2}=\frac{1}{2}$
