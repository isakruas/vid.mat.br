1. Trace a curva $\left\lbrace \begin{matrix} x= e^t\cos t \\ y=e^t\sin t \end{matrix}\right.$, $0\leq t\leq\pi$ e calcule seu comprimento.

$\displaystyle L=\int_0^{\pi}\sqrt{\left(e^t\cos t -\sin t\right)^2 \left(e^t\sin t e^t\cos t \right)^2}\,dt$

$\displaystyle\;\;\;=\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{e^{2t}\cos t-2e^{2t}\sin t e^{2t}\sin^2 t e^{2t}\sin^2 t 2e^{2t}\sin t\cos t e^{2t}\cos t}\,dt$

$\displaystyle\;\;\;=\int_0^{\pi}\sqrt{2e^{2t}}\,dt=\int_0^{\pi}\sqrt{2}e^t\,dt=\sqrt{2}\left(e^{\pi}-1\right)$
